Trash Trove! being sunset

Farewell.

It's with a heavy heart that I announce that Trash Trove!'s sunsetting. The game servers will officially go offline on the 29th of December, 2024.

Why's this happening?

Trash Trove! was originally made as a fun school project, built and developed by the support of you guys. Recently, Trash Trove! has become increasingly hard to maintain, coupled with server issues that make the experience less and less fun.

Along with the fact that Trash Trove! has already been submitted, presented, and graded as an assignment, I think that it's in my best interest to shut down the game rather than keep it online and not have time to update and maintain the game.

What Happens Next?

  • The servers will remain active until the 29th of December, 2024..
  • Top scores will be forever archived in a new Hall of Fame page.
  • Players will retain access to any downloaded content locally on their devices.
    (This means you can archive the game and still play it.)

Thank You

I'm incredibly grateful for the support all of you have shown for Trash Trove! Your support and feedback have made this journey unforgettable.

Thanks, everyone.
